Kare Kare Recipe

What is Kare Kare

Kare kare is a traditional Filipino dish, a beef stew flavored with peanut butter. It is usually accompanied by vegetables such as eggplant and cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo Beef cubes
  • 1 Egg plant
  • 1 bunch of pechay
  • 2tbsp Peanut butter
  • 5 strands of baguio beans
  • 1tsp fish sauce

Cooking Process

  1. Chop beef cubes into square chunks
  2. Chop eggplant into long strips
  3. Slow cook beef for 4hours
  4. Mix peanut butter to the broth
  5. Add vegetables
  6. Let it simmer for 10 minutes
  7. Season with fish sauce
  8. Serve
