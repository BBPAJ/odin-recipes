Adobo Recipe

What is Sinigang

sinigang is considered as the Filipino's comfort food, it is usually composed of meat, a sour paste, and vegetables. its sour taste can be attributed to tamarind. it is the perfect comfort food during a rainy day.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo pork
  • 1 packet of tamarind powder Mix
  • 1 radish
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 3 kangkong branches

Steps

  1. Saute pork
  2. Add tomatoes
  3. Add water and let the pork boil until soft
  4. Add vegetables, let it simmer until tender
  5. Season with fish sauce
  6. Serve
