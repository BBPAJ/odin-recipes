Adobo Recipe

What is Adobo

Adobo is the most popular Filipino dish, it is usually which is usually meat marinated in soy sauce and vinegar paired with potatoes or eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo chicken
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 laurel leaf
  • 2 peppercorns
  • 1 cup minced potatoes

Steps

  1. Saute chicken
  2. Add soy sauce and vinegar
  3. Add water and let it simmer fr 10 minutes
  4. Add eggs and potatoes
  5. Let it simmer for another 10 minutes
  6. Let it simmer for 10 minutes
  7. Serve
